## Quick Start (no setup required)

Open `LIMINAL_EVERMEMOS_POC.html` in any browser. No server needed — it's a self-contained HTML file.

## Navigation

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `← →` | Navigate between scenes |
| `M` | Toggle Day 1 / Day 90 (Scene 1 only) |
| Click dots (right edge) | Jump to any scene |

## Three Scenes

### Scene 1: The Ritual (Day 1 vs Day 90)

**Day 1:** Seven coherence factors scored. "Recorded." The system has nothing to say.

**Press M for Day 90:** The system makes a claim — "You've been avoiding connection for nine days." Three response options:
- **Yes** — agreement stored
- **No** — correction input appears. Type a correction. Press Enter. "Correction stored as MemCell."
- **Show me the evidence** — expands raw MemCell citations with dates

The delta between Day 1 and Day 90 IS the thesis. Memory gives the system standing.

### Scene 2: The Council

Three Jungian archetypes speak with memory of prior sessions:
- **Warrior** (rose) — confronts bluntly, references being dismissed in January
- **Sage** (violet) — confronts by questioning, asks what you actually come to it for
- **Sovereign** (teal) — confronts by waiting, earned presence

Memory annotations under each voice explain WHY this response differs from a non-memory response.

### Scene 3: The Arc (The Disputable Account)

Three months reconstructed as a disputable account:
- Three Imprint paintings (visual evidence of state change)
- Three sections: January (ENDING) → February (LIMINAL) → March (EMERGING)
- Each section has markup buttons: **Accurate** / **Wrong** / **Missing something**
- Click Wrong or Missing to see simulated corrections
